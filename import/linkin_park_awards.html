Linkin Park Awards and Accolades

Grammy Awards

  • 2002: Best Hard Rock Performance for "Crawling"
  • 2006: Best Hard Rock Performance for "Numb/Encore" (with Jay-Z)

American Music Awards

  • 2002: Favorite Pop/Rock New Artist
  • 2008: Favorite Alternative Rock Artist

MTV Video Music Awards

  • 2002: Best Hard Rock Video for "In the End"
  • 2003: Best Rock Video for "Faint"
  • 2004: Best Art Direction for "Breaking the Habit"

Billboard Music Awards

  • 2002: Top New Artist
  • 2007: Top Rock Group

World Music Awards

  • 2002: Best Selling Rock Group
  • 2005: Best Rock Group

Teen Choice Awards

  • 2002: Choice Rock Group
  • 2008: Choice Rock Song for "Shadow of the Day"

Other Notable Awards

  • 2003: Kerrang! Award for Best Live Act
  • 2010: MTV Europe Music Award for Best World Stage Performance
